Nayuta is a fair-skinned boy with short ruffled hair and purple bluish eyes. His hair is white with purple tips and sticks out from all sides of his head. Underneath his jacket, Nayuta also wears a white belt choker like Kanata. He has white shorts and socks with the cozmez logo and a pair of turquoise, blue and purple sneakers.
